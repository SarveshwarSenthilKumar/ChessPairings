Tiebreak Information
Tiebreak Order
  1. Sonneborn-Berger (SB)
    Sum of the scores of the players you've beaten, plus half the scores of those you've drawn with.
    TB1
  2. Buchholz (BH)
    Sum of the scores of all your opponents, excluding the lowest-scoring one.
    TB2
  3. Direct Encounter
    The result of the game between tied players, if applicable.
  4. Performance Rating
    The rating that would result in the player's current score against the opponents faced.
Team Standings Tiebreaks
  1. Match Points (MP)
    Total points earned in team matches (2 for win, 1 for draw, 0 for loss).
    Primary
  2. Game Points (GP)
    Sum of individual game points from all matches.
    TB1
  3. Direct Match Result
    The result between the tied teams, if they've played each other.
    TB2
  4. Buchholz (BH)
    Sum of the match points of all opponents, excluding the lowest-scoring one.
    TB3
How Team Standings Work
  • Teams are ranked by Match Points (MP) first.
  • If tied, Game Points (GP) determine the ranking.
  • If still tied, the direct match result between the teams is used.
  • If teams haven't played or drew, Buchholz is the final tiebreak.
