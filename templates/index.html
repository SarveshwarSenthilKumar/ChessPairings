How The Swiss System Works

The fair tournament format without elimination

1

Group by Score

Players are grouped into "score brackets" based on their total points after each round.

2

Sort Within Groups

Players are sorted by score, then rating, then alphabetically or at random.

3

Pair Players

Top vs bottom pairing in each group, avoiding repeat opponents and balancing colors.

4

Assign Byes

If odd number of players, assign a bye to the lowest-ranked eligible player.
